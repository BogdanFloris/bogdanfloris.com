About

I'm Bogdan, a software engineer from Bucharest, Romania. I currently work at Google on the Android windowing tools team. My day-to-day is tracing infrastructure — Perfetto and traced, with instrumentation across SurfaceFlinger, WindowManager, and Skia — and I own Flicker lib and Winscope on the side.

Before Google I spent about four years at DataCamp on the Content Platform team, enabling new technologies in the platform and maintaining the tooling content developers use to author courses. Before that, Bloomberg in London on the MARS risk platform.

Outside work I ski a lot and play guitar, electric and acoustic (badly, but happily). This site is my small piece on the internet — I keep it simple and, naturally, Gruvbox.
