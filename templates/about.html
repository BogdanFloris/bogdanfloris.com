Hello, there!

I'm Bogdan, a software engineer from Bucharest, Romania. I currently work at DataCamp, where we are building a platform which people of all backgrounds or experiences can use to learn data skills. I work there as a full stack engineer in the Content Platform team, and recently I have been working on adding AI-powered features to the platform to improve the both the learning experience and the content.

Before DataCamp, I used to work at Bloomberg LP in the MARS team, where we were building a comprehensive suite of risk management tools that enables professionals to analyze their trading and investment portfolios.

I have been programming for most of my life, having initially started with C/C++ in middle school. I then went on to do Java during my Bachelor's and Python during my Master's. I finally got to learn modern C++ during my time at Bloomberg, and ended up combining Python and web development (TypeScript + React) at DataCamp. In my spare time, I like to experiment with a lot of languages. The backend for this website is Rust with a bit of HTMX and Tailwind to make it pretty and interactive. I have written a small interpreter in Go, I like configuring my Neovim setup in Lua, and I have recently come to love the simplicity of Zig.

This website, maybe blog in the future, is my own small piece on the internet. It's pretty minimalist as I like to keep things simple. I've chosen to use Gruvbox as the website's color scheme, because I just love it and use it for everything :D.
